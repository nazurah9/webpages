My Childhood

So to start, I grew up in a barn just like the picture shown below with all the animals and it was great! I get to interact with all the cows and donkeys throughout my childhood. They even taught me how to program! Due to them not having opposable thumbs, I have to do a lot of self learning in order to get up to speed with your average programmer.

Me with my favorite barn on a Sunday afternoon

When I first saw a horse, I was flabbergasted because it is such a magnificent animal. Not every day that you get to see a horse, especially in Singapore. I can only wish that this horse can survive for long and become my best friend when I'm older. There was also an owl that taught me how to do HTML but I lost contact with him so I did not get to learn how to do animation on web pages.

My Awakening

After a certain incident happened with a donkey and a horse, I have finally realized my true calling. I am meant for things greater than just taking care of animals and learning JavaScript from a bunch of barn inhabitants. I simply cannot accept that fact that I am destined to hang around barn animals for as long as I live as that is not very radical.

I am now AWAKENED

I shall no longer tolerate these animals' shenanigans. While I am grateful to them for teaching me JavaScript, I have had enough of their pranks which they uploaded to their YouTube channel PrankNation. I will no longer be the subject of their cheeky activities. From now on, I am my own woman, I am finally free.

Nazurah fully awakened

I went on an worked with Jeff Bezos to create one of the greatest website of all time, Amazon Web Service. However, I woke up from my slumber shortly after and I realized that nothing has happened and all of these has been a dream.
